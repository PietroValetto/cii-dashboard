How the score works

plain and transparent
Every Commissioner is graded 0–100 across three things that separate a real communicator from a big name — Reach, Resonance and Quality. Open any profile to see the grade broken down, and the specific moves that would raise it.

Reach — audience

Are you building and feeding an audience? We reward a real, consistent posting rhythm — showing up is the foundation everything else builds on.

Resonance — engagement

Does the audience engage with your content? Engagement is weighted by each platform's recommendation algorithm — a share is worth more than a comment, a comment more than a like.

Quality — craft

Are you hitting the marks of a professional page — profile optimisation, clear branding, a mix of formats, and repeatable series? This is what turns a world leader into a relatable figure with a story people actually follow and engage with.

Our social-media consulting maps 1:1 to this score: a content calendar lifts Reach & Quality, reusing announcements across formats and running signature series lift Quality, and answering your community lifts Resonance. Follow the playbook, score higher next edition.
